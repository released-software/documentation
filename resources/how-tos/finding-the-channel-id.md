# Finding the Channel/Form ID

## What is the channel/form ID?&#x20;

The channel and form IDs are unique identifiers for your embeds.&#x20;

## Where can I find the channel/form ID?

* Navigate to the **Settings** in your Released workspace (accessible via the left-hand navigation)&#x20;
* Select **Widget, Page** or **Form** in the Design section&#x20;
* Click on the **Install** button at the bottom right.&#x20;
* You will find the **Channel/Form ID** near the top of the instructions.&#x20;
